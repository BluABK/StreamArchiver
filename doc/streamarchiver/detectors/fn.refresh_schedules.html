Background task: periodically refresh upcoming-stream schedules for enabled Twitch/YouTube monitors. A short poll tick picks up newly-added monitors quickly; each monitor is re-fetched at most every few hours (tracked in-memory), and fetches are de-duplicated per URL within a pass.
